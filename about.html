Sharon's Story

Sharon is a self-taught polymer clay artist based in the Netherlands, whose work has quietly found its way into homes across Europe and beyond.

Every portrait begins with a conversation and a photograph. What comes next is entirely Sharon's — hours of careful observation, sculpting, and refinement until the piece looks not just like the person, but like them.

In 2024, a celebrity in the Netherlands discovered her work and commissioned a portrait. The piece confirmed what her clients had known for years: this is not craft. It is art.
