Policies

Shipping & Returns

All portraits are shipped worldwide from the Netherlands. Shipping is included in the price of every commission.

As each piece is custom-made, I do not accept returns. If a piece arrives damaged in transit, please contact me within 48 hours with photos and I will work with you to resolve it.

Privacy Policy

I collect only the information necessary to complete your commission.

I will never sell or share your personal information with third parties. The photograph you send is deleted after the piece is complete.

Payment & Deposit

A 50% non-refundable deposit is required to begin any commission. The remaining balance is due before your portrait is shipped.

Cancellations before work begins may be eligible for a partial refund at my discretion.

Copyright & Usage Rights

Each portrait I create is an original work of art. I retain the right to photograph my work and share images on my website and social media.

You own the physical piece. You do not acquire the copyright to the artwork itself.
