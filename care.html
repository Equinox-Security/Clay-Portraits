Looking after your portrait

Care Instructions

Your clay portrait is crafted from high-quality polymer clay, hand-painted and sealed for lasting durability. With simple care it will remain beautiful for years.

Display

Display indoors away from direct sunlight and excessive humidity. Each piece comes ready to hang with a mounting kit included.

Cleaning

Dust gently with a soft dry cloth. Do not use water, cleaning products, or abrasive materials on the sculpture itself.

Handling

Handle with care. While polymer clay is durable, delicate details such as hair and fine clothing textures benefit from gentle handling. Hold the piece by its base or backing board where possible.
